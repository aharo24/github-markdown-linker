### command mode
- G drop to bottom
	- gg drop to top
- `}` drop blocks of code/p's
	- `{`
- 20J drop you 20 lines down
- u --> undo
- crl+r --> redo
- dd --> deletes row
	- also copies it to **clipboard**
- `.` --> keeps the algo going
- yy --> yanks it to **clipboard**
- p --> paste below
	- P
